Govind Ballabh Pant Institute of Engineering & Technology is an Institute with total commitment to quality and excellence in academic pursuits. The Institute was established in 1989, as an autonomous body, with a view to fulfil the ever-growing demand for well-trained professionals.  Read more


  • To produce quality manpower equipped with human and social values.
  • To provide best facilities, infrastructure and conducive environment to the students, researchers, staff and faculty members for high quality education and research across all the disciplines.
  • Integrating excellent technical skills, leadership, creativity and innovation for the benefit of mankind.
  • Promote quality research and education across all disciplines in the respective emerging areas for the sustainable development of nation, state and society.


    • To pivot for the excellence in technical education and producing globally competent professionals by building the strong teaching-learning and research environment.
